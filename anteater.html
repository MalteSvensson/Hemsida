Elephant Shrew

Elephant shrews, or jumping shrews, are small insectivorous mammals native to Africa,

belonging to the family Macroscelididae, in the order Macroscelidea,

whose traditional common English name comes from a fancied resemblance

between their long noses and the trunk of an elephant.
